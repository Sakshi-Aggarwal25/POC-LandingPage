Frequently Asked Questions

Which skills are available?

IELTS intelligence is currently available for Academic Reading and Listening.

Is this service free?

It's free to try the IELTS-style questions to check your skills. However, if you want to see explanations for your right and wrong answers with expert advice and personalised tips on how to improve, the feedback report starts from - no monthly charge

Is it a real IELTS test?

No, IELTS intelligence uses IELTS-style questions to instantly assess your ability in the skills you need for the real test. It's co-developed by Cambridge Assessment English, the organisation that produces IELTS.

How long do I have to answer the questions?

There is no time limit. You can save your progress as you go along and submit your answers later.

Will I get an IELTS band score?

No, you won't get an IELTS band score, but IELTS intelligence highlights your strengths and weakness. You'll get tips and advice on how to improve weaker areas so you can manage your study time effectively.

How will I know if I've improved?

You can check your IELTS skills multiple times and compare your scores to see if you are improving. It's important to follow the study advice in your feedback report before you try another IELTS intelligence skills check.

Do I have to download any software?

No, you don't have to download anything. Our software works in the cloud so you can access IELTS intelligence wherever you are, and on any device including mobile, tablet and desktop.
